About Us

“Small changes make a big impact—start with one green habit.”

At Plan My Event, we believe that great celebrations shouldn’t come at the cost of our planet. We are a dedicated sustainable event planning platform for colleges, designed to help student communities organize eco-friendly, cost-effective, and memorable events.

🎯 Our Mission

To empower colleges and student organizations to host events that are not only fun and engaging but also mindful of the environment. We strive to create a culture where sustainability is celebrated alongside creativity, teamwork, and innovation.

🌱 What We Do

  • Eco-Conscious Event Planning – From cultural festivals and fests to seminars and workshops, we help design events that reduce waste and conserve energy.
  • Resource Optimization – We encourage reusing, recycling, and choosing sustainable alternatives like digital invites, biodegradable decorations, and plastic-free catering.
  • Student-Friendly Approach – Our platform is simple, practical, and tailored for young organizers who want to balance creativity with responsibility.

💡 Why Choose Us?

  • We understand the pulse of college life and the importance of budget-friendly solutions.
  • We are passionate about building awareness of sustainability in every small step—whether it’s reducing paper waste, managing electricity usage, or creating recycling stations at events.
  • Every event you plan with us contributes to a larger movement for a greener future.

🌍 Our Vision

We envision a future where every campus event—big or small—becomes an opportunity to practice sustainability, inspire eco-consciousness, and make students proud contributors to a better tomorrow.
